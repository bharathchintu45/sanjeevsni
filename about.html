About Sanjeevani Hospital

Healthcare with heart, at the centre of Dharmaram since day one.

Our Story

Why "Sanjeevani"?

In our tradition, Sanjeevani is the legendary herb that restores life. That is the promise behind our name: when a family in Dharmaram needs help — at midnight, on a festival day, in the middle of harvest — care should be within reach, not a long journey away.

Located at Ambedkar Chowk on ZP High School Road, Sanjeevani Hospital serves Dharmaram and the surrounding villages of Karimnagar district with round-the-clock emergency care, general medicine and honest guidance.

We believe good treatment should never depend on how much a family can afford. Fair pricing, clear explanations and respect for every patient are the foundation of how we work.

“I am here to serve needy and poor patients.” — Dr. Prashanth, Sanjeevani Hospital
