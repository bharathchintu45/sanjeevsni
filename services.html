Our Services

Complete care for the whole family — under one roof, around the clock.

Emergency & Casualty — 24×7

Accidents, chest pain, breathing difficulty, snake bites, high fever at night — our team is ready at any hour. Come straight in or call ahead so we can prepare.

General Medicine

Consultation and treatment for fevers, infections, body pains, stomach problems, diabetes, blood pressure and other everyday and chronic conditions.

Mother & Child Care

Pregnancy check-ups, safe delivery support, newborn care, childhood illnesses and vaccination guidance for growing families.

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Blood counts, sugar and thyroid profiles, urine tests and routine investigations — with quick, dependable reports so treatment starts fast.

In-House Pharmacy

Prescribed medicines available immediately after your consultation, at fair prices — no second trip to town needed.

In-Patient Care — General Ward

A clean, spacious general ward with comfortable beds and attentive nursing for observation, IV fluids or admission.

Health Check-ups

Blood pressure, sugar and general health screening — catch problems early, before they become serious.

Operation Theatre & Procedures

A fully equipped, modern operation theatre for surgical procedures, plus dressings, suturing and minor procedures done safely.

ICU — Intensive Care

A dedicated intensive care unit with monitored beds for patients who need close, critical attention.
